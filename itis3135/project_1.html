Project Overview:

The purpose of my website will be to provide information for those looking into starting van life. The content will include a list of benefits, a slideshow of commonly chosen vehicles to equip, a slideshow of fully equipped vehicles, a list of costs to consider, and an interactive calculator to help estimate the amount of savings required depending on users desires/input.

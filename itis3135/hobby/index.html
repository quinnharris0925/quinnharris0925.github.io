Hobby Site

Home

Welcome to my hobby site! Here you can find information about my favorite hobby, lacrosse, including my personal history with the sport and some general information regarding the best sport around!!

My History

My name is Quinn and I've been playing lacrosse for over 10 years. It all started when I was in middle school when a friend introduced me to the sport. Since then, I've played on various teams as I grew up including a youth county team and my highschool team. I even played goalie for a couple seasons.

Schedule

The best time of year to play lacrosse is typically in the spring, when most organized leagues and school teams have their seasons. However, there are also summer and fall leagues available for those who want to continue playing year-round. Additionally, there are several regional tournaments throughout the year that lacrosse enthusiasts can participate in.

Where

Lacrosse is a popular sport in many regions of the United States and Canada, with strong roots in Native American culture. Some of the best places to play and learn about the sport include universities with strong lacrosse programs, such as Syracuse University and Johns Hopkins University, as well as areas with strong Native American communities, such as the Iroquois Nation.

How

If you're new to lacrosse, there are several resources available to help you get started. Many schools and community organizations offer beginner clinics and leagues for both children and adults. You can also find instructional videos and tutorials online to help you master the basics of the sport, such as passing, catching, and shooting.

Why

Lacrosse has a rich history and cultural significance, particularly among Native American communities. It was traditionally played as a form of medicine and healing, as well as for spiritual purposes. Today, it continues to be a popular sport that promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship

Vehicles for Vanlife

Van

A van is the most popular vehicle choice for vanlife. They offer ample space for living and storage, and are easy to customize for your needs. However, they can be expensive to purchase and maintain, and their size can make it difficult to park in certain areas.

Truck

A truck can offer more off-road capabilities and towing power than a van, but they have less interior space. They can also be more difficult to drive and maneuver, especially in urban areas.

Sedan

A sedan may not be the first choice for vanlife, but they can still be used for weekend camping trips or short-term travel. They offer better gas mileage and are easier to park than larger vehicles, but their size limits their storage and living space.
